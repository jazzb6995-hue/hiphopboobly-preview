Book Boobly

A live show kids will never forget 🎤

Hip Hop Boobly turns assemblies, reading days, and special events into a sing-along celebration of confidence, kindness and learning. Every visit is high-energy, age-appropriate, and built around positive messages your students will carry home.

  • Interactive sing-along and dance performance
  • Positive themes: be kind, be you, be great
  • Reading sessions and learning activities
  • Meet-and-greet with the Boobly Squad

Our Story

Music that helps kids grow 🌟

Hip Hop Boobly creates fun, educational songs and shows that inspire kids to be confident, kind and unstoppable. Every episode mixes catchy music, positive messages and learning that little ones love and parents trust.

The Hip Hop Boobly Squad

A super team of friends teaching kids around the world positive lessons.

Boobly

The star of the show! Boobly leads the squad with music, energy and big positive vibes.

Hip Hop Mimi

A young motivational speaker and Boobly's best friend. Together they make a super team.

Rockstar Kimmy

Someone has to keep up with the kids. Their teacher, Rockstar Kimmy, makes it happen.

Hip Hop Boom

A teenage music genius! Boobly and Boom make creative music that teaches kids positive lessons.

Dezzio

The O.G. of the squad. He always has good advice to share with everyone.
